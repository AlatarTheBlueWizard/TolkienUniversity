List of Some Available Courses (all semesters)

Course Name Credit Amount Course Description
Elven Speech 3 Credits Students will learn the basics of the elven language, course includes a trip to rivendell which is the home of the elves
Hunting 3 Credits Students will learn how to hunt and set up animal traps correctly, includes a hunting expedition
Archery 2 Credits Students will learn how to properly use a bow and will include vigorous training at the archery range
Horse Taming 5 Credits Students will learn how to tame wild horses and befriend them, the students are able to keep the horse once the class is completed
Leather Crafting 3 Credits Students will learn how to craft leather pouches, saddles, sheathes, etc. Everything the student crafts they are able to keep
Cooking 2 Credits Students will learn how to cook in the wilderness as well as learn what is safe to eat what is not
Rune Deciphering 5 Credits Students will learn how to decipher ancient runes, mostly appicable to wizard majoring students
Staff Crafting 3 Credits Students will learn how to create their own wizard staffs, mostly applicable to wizard majoring students
Weather Manipulation 6 Credits Students will learn how to manipulate the weather through vigorous spell training, mostly applicable to wizard majoring students
Lute 3 Credits Students will learn how to play the lute, will include a final project in which students will present a playing of ancient folktale music
Blacksmithing 4 Credits Students will learn how to smith weapons, armor, and various other sets of equipment with blacksmithing tools
Dwarven Mining 5 Credits Students will learn how to mine various caves, will include working alongside the dwarves for mithril
Swordsmanship 3 Credits Students will learn how to master the art of sword fighting, will include vigorous training in the courtyard battlegrounds
